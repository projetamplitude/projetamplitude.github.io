About us

Projet Amplitude is a booking agency located in France. Since few years now we promote Bass Music gigs in Paris (Drops of Sweat) bringing together thousands of fans in the best places of our city. We use to deal with international artists in their journey here in Paris. With our experiences we are now able to manage everything, mainly artists during their travel in Europe and France.

Our Roster

DUBSCRIBE

Dubscribe is a Dubstep producer from Tokyo. He established himself as one of the heaviest producers in bass music.

He signed on Rottun Recordings (Excision Monster Label) his 'Brainstorm EP' which went straight to the No.1 on the Beatport dubstep charts. His second Rottun release called 'Venom EP' reached No.1 on the Beatport Glitch Hop charts. He also signed to Downlink's label Uplink Audio, with 'Hyper Galaxy EP' and it reached No.3 on the Beatport dubstep charts.

In 2016, He released official remix for 'Figure' and 'KJ Sawka'. Those remix received big support from some of the industry's heaviest hitters including Destroid and are now played in their show. He also remixed one of the biggest Japanese pop singers "Ayumi Hamasaki" and gave a big impact on Japanese pop scene. No doubt this talented Japanese is becoming a prominent figure in bass music.
